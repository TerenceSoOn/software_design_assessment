## Project Specification: FlirtNet – A Supportive Social Discovery Platform

### 1. Problem Statement

Modern dating and social discovery applications have made it easier for people to meet online, but they have also created new problems. Many current platforms are built around fast swiping and shallow first impressions. As a result, users often experience anxiety and burnout instead of meaningful social connection. The main problem is these platforms focus heavily on helping users get a match, but provide little support after the match is made.
For many users, the most difficult part of online interaction is not finding another person, but knowing how to begin a conversation, maintain it respectfully, and build confidence. Some users may feel nervous about starting chats, while others may have concerns about harmful behaviour or the general safety of online communication. Existing applications often do not provide enough tools to support users in these areas, which leads to poor experiences and discourages long-term engagement.
Our group proposes FlirtNet, a dating platform designed to promote more supportive online interaction. Rather than being only as a matching application, FlirtNet aims to assist users throughout the communication process. The platform is intended to help users discover compatible people, communicate more confidently, and interact in a safer environment. In this way, the software would improve both the quality of conversation and the overall user experience.
The system should be accessible to users across common devices and should support timely interaction between matched users. It should also emphasize usability, safety, and convenience so that users can engage with the platform in a natural and low-pressure way. The requirements for this system are given in the list below as potential features for the overall system. 

### 2. Potential Features

a) **Real-Time Matching System:**
The core of the platform should be a matching system that allows users to discover others based on selected preferences such as age range, interests, and personal goals. The matching process should be efficient and responsive so that users can move smoothly from browsing to interaction. This feature is important because it is the entry point to the user experience.

b) **Random Chat Messaging:**
The system should provide a random chat function that allows users to start conversations with new people in a casual way. This feature would help users meet others beyond standard matching and encourage more interaction. Once a conversation ends, users should be given the option either to leave the conversation, or to add each other as friends if they wish to continue communicating. This design would achieve the transition from casual interaction to longer-term connection more natural and flexible.

c) **Friend-Based Private Messaging:**
If two users develop a stronger connection, the platform should allow them to add each other as friends and continue communication through private messages. This type of messaging would represent a more personal and stable form of interaction compared with random chat. It should be secure, easy to use, and suitable for longer-term communication between users.

d) **User Profiles and Preferences:**
The system should allow users to create, update, and manage their personal profiles. This may include profile photos, short self-descriptions, interests, and matching preferences. A clear profile system would help users present themselves more effectively while also improving the quality of matches generated by the platform.

e) **Public Forum:**
The platform should include a public forum where users can create posts, share opinions, ask questions, and participate in community discussions. This feature would make the application more than just a one-to-one communication tool, since it also provides a shared social space for broader interaction. It may help users feel more involved in the community and encourage continued use of the platform.

f) **AI Conversation Coach:**
A unique feature of FlirtNet could be an AI-based conversation assistant that helps users who struggle with starting or continuing conversations. During a chat, the system may suggest polite, relevant, and engaging replies based on recent context. This feature would be especially useful for users who feel shy, inexperienced, or uncertain about how to communicate effectively in online social settings.

g) **Safety Monitoring:**
To encourage a safer environment, the platform may include a safety monitoring feature that helps detect harmful, abusive, or inappropriate language in user messages. Content identified as suspicious could be flagged for review, hidden, or otherwise managed to reduce harm to users. This feature would help the system place more emphasis on healthy interaction and user protection.

h) **Practice Mode:**
The platform may also include a low-pressure practice environment where users can interact with different virtual personas before speaking to real matches. This feature could help users build confidence, improve conversation skills, and reduce anxiety. It would be particularly valuable for users who are new to social discovery apps or who feel uncomfortable beginning real conversations immediately.

i) **Cross-Platform Accessibility:**
FlirtNet should be accessible for users in both web and mobile environments. Since people often communicate in different settings throughout the day, the system should provide a consistent and user-friendly experience regardless of device. This would improve accessibility and make the platform more practical for everyday use.
