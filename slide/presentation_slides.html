The Vision & The "Why"

Speaker: Junlin Li

The Problem - "The Lonely Crowd"

  • The Paradox: We are more connected than ever, yet loneliness is rising.
  • Fragmentation:
    • Users juggle multiple apps: Tinder for dating, Reddit for forums, WhatsApp for chat.
    • There is no unified journey from "Stranger" to "Partner."
  • The "Cold" Start:
    • Traditional apps offer little guidance.
    • Shy or anxious users struggle to break the ice.

The Vision & The "Why"

Speaker: Junlin Li

Our Philosophy

  • Human-Centric Design:
    • Social First: Technology is the bridge, not the destination.
    • Supportive: We believe a platform should actively help users connect, not just match them.
  • The Goal:
    • To create a safe, unified ecosystem where users can express themselves, meet spontaneously, and build deep, lasting relationships.

The Vision & The "Why"

Speaker: Junlin Li

Project Architecture

  • MVC Pattern (Model-View-Controller):
    • Model: Database schemas and Pydantic models (SQLAlchemy).
    • View: React frontend components and pages.
    • Controller: FastAPI routers and business logic.
  • Separation of Concerns:
    • Ensures modularity and easier maintenance.
    • Frontend and backend are decoupled via RESTful APIs.

The Vision & The "Why"

Technology Snapshot

  • Built for Performance & Scale:
    • Frontend: React with Vite for a responsive UI.
    • Backend: FastAPI for high-speed async processing.
    • Real-Time: Socket.IO for instant messaging.
    • Intelligence: DeepSeek LLM for AI features.
